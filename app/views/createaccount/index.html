User testing (1)

  • Simple usability, 03/03/20, 9 sessions of 20 minutes

Key Findings (7)

  • Users need more content and explanation in terms of what NHS login is and can do
  • User mental model of NHS login is very poor - the actual conceptual understanding is not good
  • Users said they knew where to locate their NHS number
  • The process is usable - there were no usability issues
  • Users thought that the example login button was real
  • Users expect a more personalised email
  • When encountering an error users would expect guidance to recover, or they would ring the GP for help

Design Challenges (4)

  • How can we explain upfront what NHS login is and what it can do?
  • How can we avoid users having to provide their NHS number to complete the journey
  • How can we give an example of how to use login without looking like a clickable button
  • How can we make the email more personalised - adding content about apps they have been asked to download?
